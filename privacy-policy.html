Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

BookYourService ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

  • Identity Information: Name, phone number, and KYC documents (for service providers).
  • Location Information: Real-time location to match users with nearby service providers.
  • Service Information: Details of the services booked and provided.

Data Safety

We do not sell your personal data. All sensitive financial information is processed through secure, encrypted gateways. Geolocation data is only used during active booking sessions.

Compliance

We comply with standard data protection regulations regarding the storage and processing of PII.
