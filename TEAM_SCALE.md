## 📅 TEAM MANAGEMENT

### **Daily Standups (10:00 AM)**
- **Duration:** 15 minutes
- **Format:** Each team member shares:
  - What did you work on yesterday?
  - What will you work on today?
  - Do you have any blockers?
- **Tools:** Slack/Zoom

### **Weekly Sprints (Monday - Friday)**
- **Duration:** 1 week
- **Planning:** Monday - Sprint planning and task assignment
- **Review:** Friday - Sprint review and retrospectives
- **Tools:** Jira/Trello

### **Monthly Reviews (Last Friday of month)**
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Format:**
  - Review monthly metrics and KPIs
  - Discuss successes and failures
  - Plan next month priorities
  - Discuss team feedback
- **Tools:** All dashboards and analytics

### **Quarterly Planning**
- **Duration:** 4 hours
- **Format:**
  - Review quarterly goals and achievements
  - Review market trends and opportunities
  - Plan next quarter priorities
  - Discuss team growth and scaling
- **Tools:** All data and analytics

## 🎯 TEAM HIRING PROCESS

### **Recruitment Channels**
- **LinkedIn:** Search for candidates
- **Wellfound:** Post job descriptions
- **AngelList:** Post job descriptions
- **Twitter:** Share job postings
- **Facebook Groups:** Share in developer groups
- **Referrals:** Employee referrals
- **Campus Hiring:** Top engineering colleges

### **Recruitment Process**
1. **Application Review:** Review resumes and applications
2. **Screening Call:** 15-30 minute screening call
3. **Technical Assessment:** 2-3 hour technical assessment
4. **Team Interview:** 1-2 hour team interview
5. **Offer:** Make job offer
6. **Onboarding:** 1-2 week onboarding process

### **Onboarding Process**
- **Week 1:**
  - Company introduction and culture
  - Team introductions
  - Tools and accounts setup
  - Code and documentation access
  - Training on tools and processes
  - First tasks assigned
- **Week 2:**
  - Shadow senior team members
  - Complete first independent tasks
  - Code reviews and feedback
  - Regular check-ins
