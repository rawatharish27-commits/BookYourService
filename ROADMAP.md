
# 🧩 BookYourService: Strategic Roadmap

## 🎯 Phase 1: Operational Scaling (Month 1-3)
*   **Mobile Experience:** Launching high-performance React Native apps for both Clients and Professionals.
*   **Geo-Fencing:** Real-time professional tracking to provide "Pro Arriving" updates to clients.
*   **Automated Payouts:** Scheduled weekly settlements integrated with bank APIs.

## 🧠 Phase 2: Intelligent Marketplace (Month 4-6)
*   **AI Support Bot (Gemini API):** 24/7 automated resolution for basic booking changes and FAQ queries.
*   **Smart Matching 2.0:** Use Gemini to analyze professional reliability scores and client feedback to optimize the auto-assignment engine.
*   **Surge Pricing Logic:** Dynamic price adjustments based on real-time demand in specific zones (e.g., high demand for AC repair in summer).

## 🚀 Phase 3: Ecosystem Expansion (Month 7-12)
*   **Enterprise Portal:** White-labeled service management for housing societies and commercial complexes.
*   **BYS Academy:** In-app training and certification modules for service professionals to upskill.
*   **Multi-Region Support:** Deployment across Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities with regional language support.

---
**Vision:** To become the world's most trusted and friction-less service delivery infrastructure.
